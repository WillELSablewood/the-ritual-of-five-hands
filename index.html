The Ritual of Five Hands

A five-sign duel of chance, pattern and intent.

How to Play

The Ritual of Five Hands is based on the expanded Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock ruleset. Each round, you and the computer each choose one of five symbolic moves: Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard or Spock.

Rock crushes Scissors and Lizard. Paper covers Rock and disproves Spock. Scissors cut Paper and decapitate Lizard. Lizard eats Paper and poisons Spock. Spock smashes Scissors and vaporises Rock.

Your goal is simple: win more rounds than the computer before the ritual ends. You decide how long the ritual lasts by choosing a short, standard or long game above.
